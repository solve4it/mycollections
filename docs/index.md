# Welcome to MyCollections

MyCollections is a local-first personal collection management app with a plugin architecture. Catalog vinyl, LEGO sets, audio gear, trading cards, or anything else you collect — with purpose-built plugins for each type of thing, and optional cloud sync when you want your data on more than one device.

## What you can do

- **Catalog what you collect** with plugin-specific fields, metadata, and imagery
- **Add items quickly** via barcode and image scanning
- **Search and filter** across every collection at once, or drill down inside one
- **Enrich** your catalog from external sources (catalogs, marketplaces, databases)
- **Sync** across devices when you opt in, or stay fully offline
